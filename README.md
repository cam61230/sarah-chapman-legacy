# DJ Sarah Chapman Legacy Site

A tribute website celebrating Sarah Chapman's pioneering career in 1990s Brighton house music and her journey to becoming a commercial airline pilot.

## About Sarah

Sarah Chapman was one of the first female DJs on the UK dance music scene during the golden age of house music in Brighton. Now a qualified commercial pilot, her story is one of breaking barriers and reaching new heights—both on the dance floor and in the skies.

## Website Features

- **Timeline**: Key moments in Sarah's career
- **Flyer Archive**: Original promotional materials from the 90s
- **Photo Gallery**: Performance shots and behind-the-scenes memories  
- **Legacy Section**: Her impact on dance music history

## Built With

- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- 90s-inspired neon design aesthetic
- Responsive design for all devices
- Hosted on GitHub Pages at djsarahchapman.com
