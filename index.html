About

From Decks to Flight Decks

Sarah Chapman was one of the pioneering female DJs in the UK house music scene during the 1990s, making her mark in Brighton's legendary club culture before transitioning to become a commercial airline pilot.

The Brighton Years

During the golden age of house music, Sarah was breaking barriers and moving dance floors across Brighton's most iconic venues. Her sets were legendary, her presence commanding, and her contribution to the scene immeasurable.

Taking Flight

After dominating the decks, Sarah set her sights on the skies, becoming a commercial airline pilot. From commanding dance floors to commanding aircraft, her journey exemplifies determination and excellence.
